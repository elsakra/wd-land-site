# Essential Due Diligence Checklist for Land Purchases

Purchasing land is a significant investment that requires careful investigation. Our comprehensive due diligence checklist helps ensure you make informed decisions and avoid costly mistakes.

## Legal and Title Research

### Title Investigation
- [ ] Clear title verification
- [ ] Liens and encumbrances review
- [ ] Easement identification
- [ ] Boundary disputes history
- [ ] Chain of title examination

### Legal Compliance
- [ ] Zoning regulations review
- [ ] Building restrictions analysis
- [ ] Environmental regulations compliance
- [ ] Local ordinances verification

## Physical Property Assessment

### Survey and Boundaries
- [ ] Professional survey completion
- [ ] Corner markers verification
- [ ] Access rights confirmation
- [ ] Neighboring property relationships

### Environmental Factors
- [ ] Soil composition analysis
- [ ] Water rights and availability
- [ ] Flood zone designation
- [ ] Environmental hazards assessment
- [ ] Wildlife habitat considerations

## Financial Considerations

### Cost Analysis
- [ ] Purchase price evaluation
- [ ] Closing costs estimation
- [ ] Property tax assessment
- [ ] Development costs projection
- [ ] Financing options review

### Investment Potential
- [ ] Market value trends
- [ ] Appreciation potential
- [ ] Income generation possibilities
- [ ] Exit strategy planning

## Utilities and Infrastructure

- [ ] Electricity availability
- [ ] Water and sewer access
- [ ] Internet and telecommunications
- [ ] Road access and maintenance
- [ ] Proximity to services

## Professional Team Assembly

Successful land acquisition requires expert guidance:

- Real estate attorney
- Licensed surveyor
- Environmental consultant
- Tax advisor
- Local real estate professional
